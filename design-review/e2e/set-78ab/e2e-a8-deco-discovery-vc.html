QAFIYAH
عَلَى قَدْرِ أَهْلِ العَزْمِ تَأْتِي العَزَائِمُ
وَتَأْتِي عَلَى قَدْرِ الكِرَامِ المَكَارِمُ
Determination comes in measure to those who possess it, and noble deeds come in measure to the noble.
'ala qadri ahli al-'azmi ta'ti al-'aza'imu / wa ta'ti 'ala qadri al-kirami al-makarimu

Al-Mutanabbi
