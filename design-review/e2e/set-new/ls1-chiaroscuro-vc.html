poetry بالعربي
أَنا البَحرُ في أَحشائِهِ الدُرُّ كامِنٌ
فَهَل سَأَلوا الغَوّاصَ عَن صَدَفاتي
I am the sea, in my depths pearls dwell. Have they asked the diver about my shells?
ana al-bahru fi ahsha'ihi ad-durru kaminun / fahal sa'alu al-ghawwasa 'an sadafati

Ahmad Shawqi
