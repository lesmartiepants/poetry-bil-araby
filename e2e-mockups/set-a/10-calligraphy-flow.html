Mahmoud Darwish • Modern
على هذه الأرض ما يستحقّ الحياة
على هذه الأرض سيّدة الأرض
On this earth there is that which deserves life,
on this earth, the lady of the earth.
ʿalā hādhihi l-arḍi mā yastaḥiqqu l-ḥayāt

Darwish is the most beloved modern Arabic poet. This excerpt from "On This Earth" is a manifesto of beauty and resistance, finding in small earthly joys the reasons for living.
