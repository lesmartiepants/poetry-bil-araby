QAFIYAH
Chapter 2
Nizar Qabbani -- A Quiet Love
أُحِبُّكِ حِينَ تَغِيبِينَ شَيْئًا فَشَيْئًا
uhibbuki hina taghibina shay'an fa shay'an
كَأَنَّكِ فِي السَّفَرِ تَتَعَلَّمِينَ نُطْقِي
ka'annaki fi al-safari tata'allamina nutqi
I love you as you fade little by little, as if in travel you learn my speech.
