المتنبي

Al-Mutanabbi
عَلَى قَدْرِ أَهْلِ العَزْمِ تَأْتِي العَزَائِمُ وَتَأْتِي عَلَى قَدْرِ الكِرَامِ المَكَارِمُ
According to the people of resolve come the resolves, and according to the noble come the noble deeds.
Ala qadri ahli al-azmi ta'ti al-aza'imu / wa ta'ti ala qadri al-kirami al-makarimu
