Abu al-Atahiya • Ascetic Poetry
الناسُ في غَفَلاتِهِم
وَرَحى المَنيَّةِ تَطحَنُ
People are lost in their distractions,
while the mill of death keeps grinding.

✨ Abu al-Atahiya pioneered zuhdiyyat (ascetic poetry). This stark couplet juxtaposes human heedlessness with the relentless passage of mortality — the grinding mill metaphor is devastatingly effective.
