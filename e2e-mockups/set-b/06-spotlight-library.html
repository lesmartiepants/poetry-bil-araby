Badr Shakir al-Sayyab • Modern
عيناكِ غابتا نخيلٍ ساعةَ السَحَر
أو شُرفتان راحَ ينأى عنهما القمر
Your eyes are two palm groves at the hour of dawn,
or two balconies from which the moon recedes.

✨ Al-Sayyab launched the free verse movement in Arabic poetry. "Rain Song" (1954) broke the qasida meter, opening Arabic poetry to modernist forms while retaining its musical power.
