## Copy Analysis

### Use of Scientific Metaphor

The walkthrough uses physics concepts to explain poetry:

| Concept | Physics | Poetry Application |
|---------|---------|-------------------|
| **Particles** | Atoms/molecules | Words forming verses |
| **Superposition** | Quantum state | Multiple meanings |
| **Field disturbance** | Wave propagation | Poet's influence |
| **Resonance** | Wave interaction | Echoes through time |
| **Gravitational center** | Mass attraction | Poet's influence |
| **Network nodes** | Connected system | Verses linking |

### Why This Works

1. **Universal language**: Physics metaphors are cross-cultural
2. **Ancient wisdom**: Arabs pioneered mathematics and astronomy
3. **Timeless connection**: Science and poetry both seek truth
4. **Tangible abstract**: Makes poetry's influence feel concrete
5. **Modern/ancient bridge**: Contemporary physics meets classical poetry
