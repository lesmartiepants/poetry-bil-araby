Ibn Zaydun • Andalusian
إِنّي ذَكَرتُكِ بِالزَهراءِ مُشتاقاً
وَالأُفقُ طَلقٌ وَمَرأى الأَرضِ قَد راقا
I remembered you in al-Zahra, longing,
the horizon clear and the earth's face radiant.

✨ Ibn Zaydun's poem to Princess Wallada is the pinnacle of Andalusian love poetry. Al-Zahra (the Brilliant) was the Umayyad palace in Cordoba, destroyed by the time of this poem — making it both love letter and elegy.
