Typography Sprint — Option B
Calligraphic Contrast
Arabic title: Lalezar (bold calligraphic display) — highest drama, maximum presence, distinct from every other text on screen.
Arabic poet: Alexandria weight 300 (geometric sans) — the narrowness and lightness create maximum contrast against Lalezar's bold strokes.
English line: a pill badge with explicit border, split bi-part attribution (gold poet / muted title) — enclosed structure signals "label" rather than "line of prose".
