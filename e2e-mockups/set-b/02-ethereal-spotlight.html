Nizar Qabbani • Modern
حبيبتي ضوء قمرٍ جديد
وعندما تمشي تمشي الورود
My beloved is the light of a new moon,
and when she walks, flowers walk with her.

✨ Nizar Qabbani revolutionized Arabic love poetry by making it direct, sensual, and accessible. His imagery of moonlight and flowers draws from both classical Arabic tradition and Romantic Western poetry.
