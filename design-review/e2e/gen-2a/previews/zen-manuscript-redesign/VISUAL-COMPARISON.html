Zen Manuscript - Redesign Alternatives

Replacement splash screens and main app screens for the Zen Manuscript theme

Context

The original Zen Manuscript had a splash screen, walkthrough, and main app. The reviewer kept only the walkthrough/onboarding flow and requested new alternatives for the splash and main app components. Below are 3 splash options and 3 main app options, all matching the Zen Manuscript aesthetic: water ripple, ink wash, washi paper, stillness.

Splash Screen Replacements

Option A: Ink Drop

Minimal splash with a single ink droplet falling into still water. Concentric rings expand outward from the impact point. Brand name fades in at center. Auto-advances after ~3 seconds.

  • Droplet + impact animation
  • 3 expanding ripple rings
  • Gold progress bar at bottom
  • Auto-advance with no user interaction

Option B: Calligraphy Brush Stroke

Brand name appears on a parchment background as a horizontal ink brush stroke draws across the page. A small gold seal/chop mark stamps below. Warm, physical, manuscript feel.

  • Washi paper texture background
  • Animated horizontal brush stroke
  • Gold chop/seal mark animation
  • SVG displacement filter for rough ink edges

Option C: Zen Garden Rake

Parallel zen garden rake lines animate across a dark sand surface. Brand emerges in a circular void between the lines, surrounded by concentric rake circles. Ultra-minimal, contemplative.

  • 15 parallel rake lines staggered entry
  • Central void with concentric circles
  • Sand grain noise texture overlay
  • Darkest, most meditative feel

Main App Replacements

Option A: Zen Reading Room

Poem centered on an ink-wash gradient background with no visible card container. Controls float as translucent circles at the bottom edge. Ultra-minimal, immersive reading experience.

  • No card/container -- text breathes freely
  • Floating circular controls with backdrop blur
  • Ghost brand in top-left at low opacity
  • "Swipe for next" text hint on right edge

Option B: Water Reflection

Poem card floats on a subtle ripple canvas with a gentle bob animation. Bottom controls are styled as rounded pebbles/stones. Maintains the original water theme most directly.

  • Live ripple canvas (click to add ripples)
  • Glassmorphism card with backdrop blur
  • Gentle floating animation on card
  • Pebble-shaped controls with labels

Option C: Manuscript Page

Clean parchment page with ink calligraphy styling. Fold line down center, aged corner marks, decorative ornament. Bottom strip uses the wooden toolbox style. Most traditional/physical feel.

  • Parchment texture with fold shadow
  • Corner marks and paper edge effects
  • Bismillah-style ornament divider
  • Page number and page-turn edge hint

Recommendations

Best Splash + Main Pairing (Cohesive): Splash A (Ink Drop) + Main B (Water Reflection) -- both share the water/ripple motif and dark palette, creating the most unified experience.

Most Immersive Reading: Main A (Zen Reading Room) -- removes all visual barriers between the reader and the poem. Best for users who want pure poetry focus.

Most Unique/Distinctive: Splash C (Zen Garden) -- the rake-line animation is unlike anything standard. Pairs well with Main A for a fully minimal zen experience.

Most Traditional/Warm: Splash B (Calligraphy) + Main C (Manuscript Page) -- both use the parchment palette and physical metaphors (brush, paper, wood). Feels like opening an actual book.

Best for Mobile: Main A (Zen Reading Room) -- fewest visual elements competing for space. Controls are compact and centered.
