Al-Khansa • Elegy
يا عَينِ جودي بِدَمعٍ مِنكِ تَهتانِ
وَلا تَمَلّي مِنَ البُكاءِ وَالأَحزانِ
O eye, be generous with your weeping tears,
and never tire of crying and of grief.
✨

Al-Khansa is the most celebrated female poet of the pre-Islamic era. Her elegies for her brother Sakhr are among the most powerful expressions of grief in world literature.
