بيت ١ من ٦ · AL-MUTANABBI
على قدر أهل العزم تأتي العزائم
وتأتي على قدر الكرام المكارم
To the measure of resolute men come resolutions,
and noble deeds arise to match the nobility of souls.
وتعظُم في عين الصغير صغارُها
وتصغر في عين العظيم العظائم
What is small appears great to the small-souled,
and what is great appears small to the great.

BRASS DECK — Design Philosophy
Precision audio instrument as UI language. Every surface reads as a machined faceplate:
hairline tick marks, monospaced micro-labels, gold-on-graphite material hierarchy.
The transport morph is the signature moment — Listen collapses in place into a real
three-key player. Right button always primary. No decorative noise.
