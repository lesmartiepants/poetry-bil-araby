Antarah ibn Shaddad • Mu'allaqa
هَل غادَرَ الشُعَراءُ مِن مُتَرَدَّمِ
أَم هَل عَرَفتَ الدارَ بَعدَ تَوَهُّمِ
Have the poets left anything unsung?
Or did you recognize the dwelling after long doubt?

Antarah opens his Mu'allaqa with a rhetorical challenge to all poets before him. As an enslaved warrior who earned freedom through valor, his poetry carries both defiance and deep longing.
