Al-Mutanabbi • Diwan
أنا الذي نظر الأعمى إلى أدبي
وأسمعت كلماتي من به صممُ
I am the one whose literature the blind can see,
Whose words reach even the ears of the deaf.
✨

Al-Mutanabbi uses hyperbole (مبالغة) here, a hallmark of classical Arabic rhetoric. The couplet establishes his supreme confidence in his craft.
