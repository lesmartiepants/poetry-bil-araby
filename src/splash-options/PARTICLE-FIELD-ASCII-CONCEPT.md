## Conclusion

The Particle Field is no longer just a visual effect—it's a **philosophical framework** made tangible through interactive design. Every visual element reinforces the central metaphor:

**Poetry = Interconnected particles of wisdom resonating across time**

This transforms the onboarding from "here's how to use the app" into "here's how to think about Arabic poetry."
