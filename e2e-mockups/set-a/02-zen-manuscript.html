Abu Nuwas • Wine Poetry
دع عنك لومي فإن اللوم إغراء
وداوني بالتي كانت هي الداء
Leave off your blame, for blame only tempts me more,
And cure me with the very thing that was my disease.
▶ Listen♡ Save↺ NextAa Size

Abu Nuwas subverts the traditional nasib (love prelude) by replacing the beloved's memory with wine. This bold opening became one of the most quoted lines in Arabic literature.
